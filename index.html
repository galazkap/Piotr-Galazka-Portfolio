Hi, everyone!

My name is Piotr and I am a student of computer science at the University of Silesia in Katowice and I specialize in computer game programming.

For as long as I can remember, there was always a computer at home, so I had contact with it every day. Of course, my favorite computer related activity was playing games, although I was more interested in how these games were made. As a child, I dreamed of becoming a developer of games that I liked so much. Now, after several years, I can say that to some extent I have managed to achieve what I have been dreaming of since I was a child.

Despite the fact that I mainly deal with the design and implementation of computer game systems, as a developer of independent games I also deal with designing levels and creating UI.
